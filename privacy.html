Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

AnyCognition Inc. ("we", "us", or "our") operates the AnyWardrobe application and website (anywardrobe.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — name, email address, and other details you provide when signing up.
  • Wardrobe data — photos and descriptions of clothing items you upload to the app.
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, including features used, session duration, and device information.
  • Communications — messages you send to us via email or support channels.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve AnyWardrobe's features and services.
  • To personalize your experience, including AI-powered outfit suggestions.
  • To communicate with you about updates, support requests, and product news.
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal information. Wardrobe photos and data are associated with your account and are not shared publicly.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services (such as cloud hosting and analytics providers) to operate AnyWardrobe. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our active systems within 30 days.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to improve your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Children's Privacy

AnyWardrobe is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
